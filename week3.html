Prototyping & Development

Not that many issues so far

Mohammad Hafeez  •  Nov 14  •  1 min read

This is the week when we finally started to develop our website. We first created a prototype on figma.com so that our team had a pretty good idea on what was going on. Thus, we had a reliable guide which we could follow and ensure there were no deviations to our design.

Once our prototype was finalized, we started on our application development. We used a cloud storage called firebase to ensure that any changes are synced across all our devices. During this week, I personally created the sign in and sign up function with data validation to allow users to access our website either as suppliers or buyers.

During this week, we fulfilled user stories 1 to 3 and were on schedule to complete the remaining sprints by week 4.
